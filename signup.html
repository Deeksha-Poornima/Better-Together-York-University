We see you

That feeling you can’t quite
name? We know it well.

It hits at different times. Sometimes it’s a Sunday afternoon when your roommate goes home for the weekend and the apartment goes quiet. Sometimes it’s watching everyone else seem to know exactly what they’re doing when you feel like you’re barely keeping up.

Homesickness isn’t just missing your family’s cooking. It’s missing the version of yourself who felt at home somewhere. It’s rebuilding that from scratch in a new city, with new people, under real pressure.

You’re not struggling because something is wrong with you. You’re struggling because this is genuinely hard — and most people don’t talk about how hard it really is.

Better Together exists so you don’t have to figure it out alone.

🏠

Two ways to feel less alone

Both are free. Both are open to everyone. Come once, come weekly — it’s entirely up to you.

Outdoor Activities

Get outside. Breathe. Reset.

Weekly activities designed to get you out of your head and into the world. No experience needed — just show up and see how you feel after.

Nature hikes on local trails
Campus picnics & social walks
Sunrise walks & weekend bike rides
Held after class & on weekends
Weekly Group Circle

Say the thing you haven’t said.

A small, confidential group where students share what’s really going on. Homesickness, stress, relationships, uncertainty — all of it is welcome here.

8–12 students max per session
Completely confidential
No pressure to share — listening is enough
Facilitated, warm, zero judgment
